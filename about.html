About Us

Igniting Change, One Heart at a Time

At Serve the Streets, we believe in the power of human connection and the profound impact of compassionate action. Our journey began on the bustling streets of Karen-Juja Road, where the stark realities of homelessness and hunger became a vivid reminder of the pressing need for change.

Explore our initiatives, join our events, and be part of a community that believes in the power of serving the streets. Your involvement can be the spark that lights up someone's world.
Ready to join hands in making a lasting impact?
Welcome to Serve the Streets where every act of kindness echoes beyond the streets we tread.

Want to Know More About Serve The Streets?

@ Juma Foundation.

Felix founded "Serve The Streets" to provide opportunities for the homeless category in Nairobi .The goal was not only to provide food and shelter ,but to give them a platform and voice to reach out to the world. As a young Philanthropist, Felix is passionate to the youths and thus try to give young people the chance to grow . Felix established "STS" to provide the concurrent feeding of the Street families if not the daily feeding and most importantly giving the street families hope for a better future.

🚀 The Spark of Inspiration
"Originating from my daily commute along Karen-Juja Road, passing by St. Teresa's to MLANGO KUBWA roundabout, I witnessed fellow brothers and sisters sleeping in the cold, waking up hungry. Inspired by their resilience, my journey with Juma Foundation began — a wake-up call to serve the homeless."
